It all started with a tiny restaurant created by foodies for fellow foodie friends

Our story

Even after 125 renowned restaurants and confectionaries and eight award winning cuisines, we are proud to say it still remains a Restaurant Group by and for the foodies

With the opening of its first outlet in 1994 in Mumbai (India), Speciality Group of Restaurants began its journey of serving authentic world cuisines and over 300 years old delectable Indian cuisines.

Our mission & vision

30 + Years Legacy 126 outlets

Within a short span of over a decade, it created history in terms of standardising recipes and creating a chain of fine dining, casual dining, bar & lounge and bakery & confectionery outlets and restaurants all over the country crossing the magical figure of 100 restaurants and confectioneries (currently 126).

11 cities in India 3 countries

We are now feeding foodies in over 121 Outlets (82 Restaurants + 39 Confectionery outlets + 7 standalone Cloud kitchens) across 17 cities in India + 2 each in UAE, Oman and London).
