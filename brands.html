From sourcing ingredients from far-off lands to attentive service, it’s all done with one objective - to give diners an experience they will cherish forever

Our iconic brands

A single-minded mission to make you feel special. It is the group's mission to share with diners authentic and unique cuisines from the world over. The group has always striven to go to great lengths to pamper and surprise patrons and ensure that every dining experience they have, is something they would remember. What really drives everyone responsible for the continuing success though, is rather simple. A love for food.

Our Chefs at Mainland China excel at preparing authentic dishes that delight the taste buds. Today, Mainland China has a countrywide presence in the form of 23 outlets across 10 cities in India, Mumbai, Pune, Kolkata, Bhubaneshwar, Chennai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Ahmedabad, Vadodara, Chandigarh.

It goes without saying that one can enjoy the most sumptuous bite of history and heritage, only at Oh! Calcutta. Our master chefs researched and unearthed those authentic recipes from the dusty shelves of libraries to unlocking grandmother's secret recipe diary. Since then, Oh! Calcutta has been one of the most talked about traditional fine dine restaurants. Present in cities: Mumbai, Kolkata, Bengaluru, Hyderabad & Delhi.

Welcome to a culinary journey through the food streets of Asia! Experience the ambient set up and the 'open live kitchen view', a first of its kind, where our Master Chefs braise, blend, toss, temper and whip up signature delicacies inspired from regions of Asia and beyond. Asia Kitchen by Mainland China promises a gastronomic adventure into the streets of Asia and beyond. Present in cities: Mumbai Pune Kolkata Gurugram Chennai Dubai & Muscat.

At Sigree, we have adapted this art of slow cooking over charcoal, a signature method for preparing food in the kitchens of Imperial India! The subtle lighting suited with the ambient decor will definitely transport you to the era of the Nawabs and Sultans, where the sound of the ghungroo, table and the Sarangi will reverberate in unison to glory. Present in Mumbai.

Experience a Royal Dining Experience like no other as we take you on a culinary journey fit for royalty. From the opulent surroundings to the tantalising flavours that grace your plate, every detail is meticulously crafted to transport you to a realm of gastronomic delight. Come get immersed in the grandeur of Indian Fine Dining. Present in Kolkata.

Café Mezzuna, a unit of Speciality Restaurants Limited, currently having two outlets in Kolkata, which explores the world of Mediterranean, Italian, Spanish and French flavours. The restaurant has a plush laid back setting perfect for lazy dinners and extended lunches where the conversation flows in no particular sequence as does the food chosen and served. It will remind you of the trattorias of Italy, and the lazy afternoons by the blue Mediterranean! Present in Kolkata.

Inspired by Calcutta's 300 year old heritage, Chourangi features street food inspired dishes and sharing plates that balance authenticity with a modern set and sensibility.

Fusing a legacy that has been around for three decades with innovation & all things new, Episode One is a one of a kind resto-bar serving delectable food from all over the world along with some beautifully crafted taptails. Welcome to Episode. Season One. Episode One.

Some say they are God's creations. Some say they are works of art. Created by Bengal's finest master craftsmen, Sweet Bengal's legacy dates back to 1995. Since then every delicacy created has brought in a special and unique taste along with the heritage of Calcutta's historic nuances. Present in Mumbai (34 outlets).

An innovative culinary adventure crafted with the flavours of Asia, that resonates with the holistic energies just like when hear the sound of a gong! A modern take on traditional Japanese temple architecture that recreates itself from a quiet dining to a pumped-up high-energy bar. Just like when we hear the sound of Gong! Present in Pune.

Step into a world inspired by the colourful chaos of Asia’s most exciting street markets, from the sizzling woks of tantalising Thailand to the bustling neon-lit streets of exotic Japan. One minute you’re in Malaysia, the other in China, Korea or Vietnam. The choice is yours! Eat all you want, as much as you want. Multiple live kitchens, a massive Asian buffet, and a walk-in bottle bar, all under one big roof - is it unusual? Not just! It’s totally bizarre!
Enjoy the fun, the flavour, and the madness. Enjoy Bizarre Asia - from the house of Speciality Restaurants, renowned for over three decades for bringing you Mainland China, Asia Kitchen and Haka. Present in Kolkata.

Café Mezzuna unveils Siciliana at Forum Mall, Kolkata — a refined tribute to Southern Italy’s rich culinary heritage. Designed with Sicilian minimalism, it offers bold, earthy flavours inspired by Sicily, Sardinia, and Calabria. In a setting of sunlit elegance and Mediterranean charm, the menu celebrates bold, authentic flavours, fine Sicilian wines, and timeless desserts, offering diners an evocative journey through Sicily’s rustic soul.
