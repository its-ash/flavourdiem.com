Creating food experiences that make people feel special

Welcome to Speciality Restaurants Limited

Our single minded commitment to bring authentic and unique cuisines from over the world served with warmth and hospitality to our guests has stood the test of time. 25 years later, we still continue to enchant our diners with flavour, formats and an elevated food experience across all our concepts. So it is natural that we don’t settle on our success and continually push our boundaries to innovate and find new and sustainable opportunities to source, partner and create food experiences that are focused on just that – making people feel special.

Our vision

To become a respected and renowned global brand

that serves the best international cuisines through our fine and casual dining restaurants bars, lounges, bakery and confectionery concepts spread across the world.

Our mission

To deliver unforgettable dining experiences to our guests through exceptional food, service and hospitality

3 drives of our mission

People: We are committed to providing our staff with the training and opportunities they need to grow and also prioritize their personal well-being.

Innovation: We are constantly curious about new developments in the industry, staying up-to-date on trends, tastes, technologies and new perspective to help us innovate.

Excellence & Ethics: We create a culture of excellence in everything we do, from the quality of our food and beverages to the ethical conduct of our business.

Our Story

It all started with a tiny restaurant created by foodies for fellow foodie friends

And even after 130 renowned restaurants and confectionaries and eight award winning cuisines, we are proud to say it still remains a Restaurant Group by and for the foodies. With the opening of its first outlet in 1994 in Mumbai (India), Speciality Group of Restaurants began its journey of serving authentic world cuisines and over 300 years old delectable Indian cuisines.

We are now feeding foodies in over 130 Outlets (72 Restaurants + 41 Confectionery outlets + 17 standalone Cloud kitchens) across 17 cities (14 in India + 1 each in UAE, Tanzania and London).

Our leadership

Anjan Chatterjee

Chairman & Managing Director

“I believe that the mantra to happiness and the spread of warmth can be achieved when good food is served with utmost love and passion. Our first outlet 'Only Fish' was founded in 1992 to recreate the magic of Calcutta cuisine in the by lanes of Mumbai. With the immense popularity that the restaurant enjoyed, I realized that there was a dearth of speciality restaurants both in the Fine Dining and Casual Dining spheres. All this motivated me to follow my single-minded dream with 'passion, 'hard work' and 'dedication' and thus, began the journey of Speciality Group of Restaurants which aimed at bringing innovative and creative dining experience for all. Our mission will always be to bring "passion onto your plate" and with our excellent team work, we continue with the spirit of "making our guests feel special."
