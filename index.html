Our Story

It all started with a tiny restaurant created by foodies for fellow foodie friends

And even after 126 renowned restaurants and confectionaries and eight award winning cuisines, we are proud to say it still remains a Restaurant Group by and for the foodies. With the opening of its first outlet in 1994 in Mumbai (India), Speciality Group of Restaurants began its journey of serving authentic world cuisines and over 300 years old delectable Indian cuisines.

We are now feeding foodies in over 126 Outlets (84 Restaurants + 42 Confectionery outlets) + 7 standalone Cloud kitchens across 11 cities in India + 2 each in UAE, Oman & London.
